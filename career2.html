MY CAREER DREAMS

Librarian & Nurse — Two passions, one purpose

Biodata Career Hobbies Skills

Librarian

I want to be a librarian to help people find knowledge. Libraries are gateways to learning, and I believe everyone should have access to information that can transform their lives.

Nurse

I want to be a nurse to care for people in need. Nursing combines scientific knowledge with compassionate care, allowing me to make a real difference in people's lives during challenging times.

My Dream

My career dream is to become both a librarian and a nurse. I love learning and helping others. These two professions allow me to do both. As a librarian, I can guide people to knowledge that empowers them. As a nurse, I can provide hands-on care that heals both body and spirit. Together, these roles represent my complete vision of service.

My Goal

I want to care for people and make a difference in their lives. Both careers let me serve people in meaningful ways. Whether through the quiet guidance in a library or the attentive care in a hospital, my goal is to touch lives positively. I believe that access to knowledge and healthcare are fundamental human needs, and I'm passionate about contributing to both.

"Two passions, one purpose" — Bridging knowledge and care

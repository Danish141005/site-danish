My Bandmate

It started as shared exhaustion on the practice field you with your colour guard flag, me with my brass instrument. For five semesters, our friendship was built in the margins: passing a water bottle under the summer sun, sharing a quiet laugh on the midnight bus after a competition, and speaking the same language of counts and crescendos.

That bond was forged in the discipline of the drill. Your perfect toss and my clean high note were our silent victories. From the awkward first formations in Semester 1 to the final, powerful performance in Semester 5, we weren't just from different sections; we were allies who created something beautiful, step by step, together.
